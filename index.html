The Process

Hand-finished. Every panel.

Detailing isn't a wash — it's a craft. Each stage is carried out by hand using professional-grade products and the right technique for your vehicle's paint and condition.

  • 01
    Inspection
    Paint depth, swirl mapping, contamination check.
  • 02
    Decontamination
    Safe multi-stage wash process, iron remover and clay bar.
  • 03
    Correction & Protection
    Machine polish — choose from wax, sealant or ceramic coating for long-term protection and shine.
Elton · Founder
About

Meet Elton

Hi, I'm Elton, the founder and detailer behind Charged Professional Detailing.

Based in Rotherham and Sheffield, I provide professional mobile detailing services throughout South Yorkshire and beyond, with the flexibility to travel wherever my customers need me.

My journey into detailing began unexpectedly after restoring and using an old pressure washer that had been thrown away. What started as simply cleaning my own vehicle quickly developed into a genuine passion for vehicle care and presentation, eventually becoming the foundation for Charged Professional Detailing.

Today, that same passion drives every service I provide. Whether it's a daily driver, a family SUV, a weekend show car, or a hardworking van, every vehicle is treated with the same level of care and attention as if it were my own.

My Standards

I believe that exceptional results come from doing things properly. That's why I've invested in professional training and am accredited to carry out machine polishing using a Dual Action (DA) polisher, ensuring paintwork enhancement and correction is completed using safe, proven techniques.

Every vehicle is treated using professional-grade equipment, carefully selected products, and safe wash methods designed to deliver effective, non-damaging results. From routine maintenance details to more advanced interior and exterior services, every stage of the process is carried out with precision, consistency, and control.

I understand that handing someone the keys to your vehicle requires trust. That's why I approach every job with the same professionalism and care that I'd expect if I were leaving my own vehicle in someone else's hands.

My Philosophy

My philosophy is simple: never rush, never cut corners, and never settle for “good enough.” Exceptional results are built through patience, precision, and attention to the smallest details.

Continuous improvement is also part of the standard. I am always developing my skills, refining my techniques, and investing in better processes to ensure every customer receives the highest level of workmanship possible.

My Promise

Whether it's a daily driver, a family SUV, a weekend toy, or a hardworking van, every vehicle receives the same meticulous approach and professional care.

Because at Charged Professional Detailing, Your Ride Is Our Priority.
